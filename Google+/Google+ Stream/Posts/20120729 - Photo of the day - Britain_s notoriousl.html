Photo of the day - Britain's notoriously inclement weather

Bad weather maybe not a good news for travelers, but it's a good opportunity for Kris Dutson scouting out the ideal locations across Britain to capture the most astonishing atmospheric shots on his camera.

Read more: http://www.dailymail.co.uk/sciencetech/article-1363789/The-cloud-chaser-Amateur-weatherman-follows-sun-compass-snap-stunning-pictures.html

BNPS.co.uk (01202 558833) Pic: Kris Dutson/BNPS.co.uk Photographer Kris Dutson has turned Britains notoriously inclement weather into a series of stunning pictures. When most people stay indoors weather nut Kris spends hours out and about waiting for the rain to clear or a break in the clouds to get the perfect shot. His pictures are not accidents - he studies meteological charts and even carries a sun compass to plan his photographs. Sunset after a storm on Portland in Dorset.
