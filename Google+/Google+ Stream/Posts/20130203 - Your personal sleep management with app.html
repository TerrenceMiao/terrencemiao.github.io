I have found my Bose clock radio a big improvement on the old alarm clock.  I set two alarms - the first plays music, and the second is set about 10 minutes later with the buzzer.  This allows me to wake up slowly, and often I'm awake before the buzzer.  The beat thing is that rather than turn on at the previously set volume, it starts at 0 and slowly increases the volume (radio & buzzer).  This avoids the sudden shock of the alarm.
