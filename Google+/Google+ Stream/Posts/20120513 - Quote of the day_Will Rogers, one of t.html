Quote of the day

Will Rogers, one of the world's best-known celebrities in the 1920s and 1930s, still could manage to share his wit and wisdom almost ninety years ago to today's politics and society.

"A fool and his money are soon elected." - splendid wit could particularly shadow the election year 2012 in countries like U.S, France, Russia, and Australia in 2013.

"If stupidity got us in this mess, why can't it get us out?" - great wisdom proofed again and again in Black Monday 1987, dot com bubble, and GFC.

Final words on men - "There are three kinds of men: The ones that learn by reading. The few who learn by observation. The rest of them have to touch an electric fence."
