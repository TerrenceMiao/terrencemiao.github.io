One feature like Logback is you can setup socket appender, for example appending to LogViewer like lilith runs on another host in logback.xml:

<appender name="lilith" class="ch.qos.logback.classic.net.SocketAppender">
<RemoteHost>your-own-place-in-the-rainbow</RemoteHost>
<Port>4560</Port>
<ReconnectionDelay>170</ReconnectionDelay>
<IncludeCallerData>true</IncludeCallerData>
</appender>
